Process Flowcharts

Five core workflows for running the office — from winning and delivering projects to approving money and hiring people. Diamonds are decisions; gold diamonds are stage gates (go/no-go sign-offs); loops show rework or nurture paths.

1

New Project Workflow

From signed SOW to closed project — mirrors the Research-Process SOP Manual, with plan, QC and senior-review gates and a healthcare training check.

flowchart TD
  A(["New project request / SOW signed"]):::se --> B["Project set-up & design"]
  B --> G1{"Plan sign-off?"}:::gate
  G1 -->|No| B
  G1 -->|Yes| C["Sampling plan · Operations"]
  C --> D["Instrument development & scripting"]
  D --> E["Pilot / soft launch"]
  E --> P1{"Pilot OK?"}
  P1 -->|No| D
  P1 -->|Yes| H{"Healthcare project?"}
  H -->|Yes| T{"PV/AE & code training current?"}
  T -->|No| TR["Train team & freelancers"]
  TR --> F["Fieldwork · Operations"]
  T -->|Yes| F
  H -->|No| F
  F --> QG{"QC & back-checks pass?"}:::gate
  QG -->|No| RR["Reject & replace data"]
  RR --> F
  QG -->|Yes| DA["Data processing & analysis"]
  DA --> RP["Reporting & recommendations"]
  RP --> SR{"Senior review passed?"}:::gate
  SR -->|No| RP
  SR -->|Yes| DL["Deliver to client & capture feedback"]
  DL --> AR["Archiving & retention"]
  AR --> Z(["Project closed"]):::se

Existing-Client Enrichment

Retention and upsell — turning a delivered project into repeat business through account planning, check-ins and nurture, feeding new SOWs back into Workflow 1.

flowchart TD
  A(["Existing client / project complete"]):::se --> B["Post-project review & feedback"]
  B --> C["Update CRM: account, satisfaction, repeat rate"]
  C --> D["Account plan: needs & whitespace · Director"]
  D --> E["Schedule check-in / QBR"]
  E --> Fq{"Opportunity identified?"}
  Fq -->|No| N["Nurture: newsletter, periodic check-in"]:::nl
  N --> E
  Fq -->|Yes| G["Draft proposal / expanded SOW · Director + BD"]
  G --> Hq{"Pricing at/above margin floor?"}
  Hq -->|No| AP["Director / Owner approval"]:::gate
  AP --> S["Send proposal"]
  Hq -->|Yes| S
  S --> W{"Won?"}
  W -->|No| L["Log reason"]
  L --> N
  W -->|Yes| K(["New SOW → New Project workflow"]):::se

4

Finance & Approval Process

Procure-to-pay with tiered approvals, separation of duties (Maker vs Checker) and Owner co-approval on banking — the core control while the Owner is abroad.

flowchart TD
  A(["Spend / purchase need"]):::se --> B{"Within approved budget?"}
  B -->|No| BA["Escalate to GM / Country Lead"]
  BA --> B2{"Budget approved?"}
  B2 -->|No| X(["Rejected"]):::se
  B2 -->|Yes| C["Raise request / PO · budget holder"]
  B -->|Yes| C
  C --> T{"Approval tier?"}
  T -->|"≤ Tier 1"| T1["Budget holder approves"]
  T -->|"Tier 1 to 2"| T2["GM + Finance approve"]
  T -->|"> Tier 2 / capex / below floor / new contract"| T3["GM + Finance + Owner sign-off"]:::gate
  T1 --> R{"Approved?"}
  T2 --> R
  T3 --> R
  R -->|No| RV["Revise / reject"]
  RV --> C
  R -->|Yes| G["Goods / services received & verified"]
  G --> M["Invoice received - 3-way match: PO, receipt, invoice"]
  M --> P["Payment prepared by Finance (Maker)"]
  P --> DD["Dual authorization - 2nd approver (Checker)"]:::gate
  DD --> O{"Above banking threshold?"}
  O -->|Yes| OW["Owner co-approval on bank"]:::gate
  OW --> EX["Payment executed"]
  O -->|No| EX
  EX --> BK["Recorded in books → monthly financial pack"]
  BK --> Z(["Closed"]):::se

5

Talent Acquisition & Onboarding

From an approved hiring need to a confirmed employee — with Owner sign-off for senior roles and the Egypt statutory onboarding steps built in.

flowchart TD
  A(["Hiring need"]):::se --> B{"Within approved headcount & budget?"}
  B -->|No| BO["Owner approval - always for senior roles"]:::gate
  BO --> B2{"Approved?"}
  B2 -->|No| X(["On hold"]):::se
  B2 -->|Yes| C["Define role / JD & band · Manager + Country Lead"]
  B -->|Yes| C
  C --> D["Source: referrals, agencies, job boards"]
  D --> E["Screen CVs → shortlist"]
  E --> Fi["Interviews · Manager + Director"]
  Fi --> Hc{"Healthcare specialist needed?"}
  Hc -->|Yes| HS["Assess specialist & compliance skills"]
  HS --> S{"Selected?"}
  Hc -->|No| S
  S -->|No| D
  S -->|Yes| SRr{"Senior role?"}
  SRr -->|Yes| OW["Owner approval of hire & comp"]:::gate
  SRr -->|No| GM["Country Lead approval"]
  OW --> OF["Offer"]
  GM --> OF
  OF --> AC{"Accepted?"}
  AC -->|No| D
  AC -->|Yes| PRE["Pre-arrival: contract 4 copies Arabic, social insurance, payroll, equipment"]
  PRE --> ON["Day 1 / Week 1: handbook, policies, confidentiality, ethics/PDPL, PV training"]
  ON --> PR["Probation - max 3 months"]
  PR --> RVw{"90-day review - confirm?"}:::gate
  RVw -->|No| EX2(["Exit per procedure"]):::se
  RVw -->|Yes| Z(["Confirmed → performance cycle"]):::se
