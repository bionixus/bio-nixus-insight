## Co-Working / Office Certificate of Insurance Requirements

Cairo co-working operators (District, GrEEK Campus, Uptown per [BIO-183 plan](/BIO/issues/BIO-183#document-plan)) typically require:

| Requirement | Standard | BioNixus action |
|---|---|---|
| General liability | $1M per occurrence / $2M aggregate | Include in GL policy |
| Additional insured | Landlord/co-working legal entity named | Request ACORD 25 endorsement at bind |
| Primary & non-contributory | Landlord's policy not excess | Standard commercial lease endorsement |
| Cancellation notice | 30 days written notice to certificate holder | Confirm with carrier |
| Workers compensation | Required if W-2 employees on-site | Waived if 1099-only / no employees (confirm with CFO) |
| Professional liability | Not always required for hot-desk | Client contracts may require separately |

**Note:** Co-working COI is satisfied by GL policy only. E&O and cyber are for client contracts, not typically landlord requirements.
