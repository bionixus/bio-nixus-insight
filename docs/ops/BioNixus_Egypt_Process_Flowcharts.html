New Project Workflow

From signed SOW to closed project — plan, QC and senior-review gates owned by Market Research Manager and Field Operations Manager; healthcare PV training gate owned by Research Compliance Specialist.

flowchart TD
  A(["New project request / SOW signed"]):::se --> B["Project set-up & design · Market Research Manager"]
  B --> G1{"Plan sign-off? · Market Research Manager"}:::gate
  G1 -->|No| B
  G1 -->|Yes| C["Sampling plan · Field Operations Manager"]
  C --> D["Instrument development & scripting · Market Research Manager"]
  D --> E["Pilot / soft launch · Field Operations Manager"]
  E --> P1{"Pilot OK? · Field Operations Manager"}
  P1 -->|No| D
  P1 -->|Yes| H{"Healthcare project?"}
  H -->|Yes| T{"PV/AE training current? · Research Compliance Specialist"}:::gate
  T -->|No| TR["Train team & freelancers · Research Compliance Specialist"]
  TR --> F["Fieldwork · Field Operations Manager"]
  T -->|Yes| F
  H -->|No| F
  F --> QG{"QC & back-checks pass? · Field Operations Manager"}:::gate
  QG -->|No| RR["Reject & replace data · Field Operations Manager"]
  RR --> F
  QG -->|Yes| DA["Data processing & analysis · Data Analyst"]
  DA --> RP["Reporting & recommendations · Market Research Manager"]
  RP --> SR{"Senior review passed? · Market Research Manager"}:::gate
  SR -->|No| RP
  SR -->|Yes| DL["Deliver to client & capture feedback · COO + Market Research Manager"]
  DL --> AR["Archiving & retention · Research Compliance Specialist"]
  AR --> Z(["Project closed"]):::se

Existing-Client Enrichment

Retention and upsell — COO owns account planning; Business Development Manager drives proposals; CEO approves below-margin pricing. Won deals feed Workflow 1.

flowchart TD
  A(["Existing client / project complete"]):::se --> B["Post-project review & feedback · Market Research Manager"]
  B --> C["Update CRM: account, satisfaction, repeat rate · Business Development Manager"]
  C --> D["Account plan: needs & whitespace · COO"]
  D --> E["Schedule check-in / QBR · COO + Business Development Manager"]
  E --> Fq{"Opportunity identified?"}
  Fq -->|No| N["Nurture: newsletter, periodic check-in · CMO"]:::nl
  N --> E
  Fq -->|Yes| G["Draft proposal / expanded SOW · COO + Business Development Manager"]
  G --> Hq{"Pricing at/above margin floor?"}
  Hq -->|No| AP["Below-floor approval · COO + CEO"]:::gate
  AP --> S["Send proposal · Business Development Manager"]
  Hq -->|Yes| S
  S --> W{"Won?"}
  W -->|No| L["Log reason · Business Development Manager"]
  L --> N
  W -->|Yes| K(["New SOW → New Project workflow"]):::se

Finance & Approval Process

Procure-to-pay aligned to spend governance — COO escalates budget variance; CFO owns tiers, maker/checker, and validation; CEO co-approves banking and board-gated spend per BIO-260 envelope.

flowchart TD
  A(["Spend / purchase need · line owner"]):::se --> B{"Within approved budget? · CFO"}
  B -->|No| BA["Escalate budget variance · COO → CEO → Board"]
  BA --> B2{"Budget approved? · Board via CEO"}
  B2 -->|No| X(["Rejected"]):::se
  B2 -->|Yes| C["Raise request / PO · budget holder (COO or CTO line)"]
  B -->|Yes| C
  C --> T{"Approval tier? · CFO"}
  T -->|"≤ Tier 1"| T1["Budget holder approves · quote-only evidence on issue"]
  T -->|"Tier 1 to 2"| T2["COO + CFO approve"]
  T -->|"> Tier 2 / capex / below floor / new contract"| T3["COO + CFO + CEO + Board confirmation"]:::gate
  T1 --> R{"Approved?"}
  T2 --> R
  T3 --> R
  R -->|No| RV["Revise / reject · CFO"]
  RV --> C
  R -->|Yes| G["Goods / services received & verified · line owner"]
  G --> M["Invoice received — 3-way match: PO, receipt, invoice · Accountant"]
  M --> P["Payment prepared · Accountant (Maker)"]
  P --> DD["Dual authorization · CFO (Checker)"]:::gate
  DD --> O{"Above banking threshold?"}
  O -->|Yes| OW["CEO co-approval on bank"]:::gate
  OW --> EX["Payment executed · Accountant"]
  O -->|No| EX
  EX --> BK["Recorded in books → monthly financial pack · CFO"]
  BK --> Z(["Closed"]):::se

Talent Acquisition & Onboarding

From an approved hiring need to a confirmed employee — CEO sign-off for senior roles and headcount overruns; HR Director and HRAdmin own Egypt statutory onboarding.

flowchart TD
  A(["Hiring need · hiring manager"]):::se --> B{"Within approved headcount & budget? · HR Director + CFO"}
  B -->|No| BO["CEO approval — always for senior roles"]:::gate
  BO --> B2{"Approved? · CEO"}
  B2 -->|No| X(["On hold"]):::se
  B2 -->|Yes| C["Define role / JD & band · HR Director + COO"]
  B -->|Yes| C
  C --> D["Source: referrals, agencies, job boards · Recruitment Specialist"]
  D --> E["Screen CVs → shortlist · HR Director"]
  E --> Fi["Interviews · hiring manager + COO"]
  Fi --> Hc{"Healthcare specialist needed?"}
  Hc -->|Yes| HS["Assess specialist & compliance skills · Research Compliance Specialist"]
  HS --> S{"Selected?"}
  Hc -->|No| S
  S -->|No| D
  S -->|Yes| SRr{"Senior role?"}
  SRr -->|Yes| OW["CEO approval of hire & comp"]:::gate
  SRr -->|No| GM["COO approval · non-senior hire"]:::gate
  OW --> OF["Offer · HR Director"]
  GM --> OF
  OF --> AC{"Accepted?"}
  AC -->|No| D
  AC -->|Yes| PRE["Pre-arrival: contract, social insurance, payroll, equipment · HRAdmin + HR Director"]
  PRE --> ON["Day 1 / Week 1: handbook, policies, ethics/PDPL, PV training · HRAdmin + Research Compliance Specialist"]
  ON --> PR["Probation — max 3 months · hiring manager"]
  PR --> RVw{"90-day review — confirm? · COO + HR Director"}:::gate
  RVw -->|No| EX2(["Exit per procedure · HR Director"]):::se
  RVw -->|Yes| Z(["Confirmed → performance cycle"]):::se
