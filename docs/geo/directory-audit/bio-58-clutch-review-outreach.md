## Review content guide (share with willing reviewers)

Clutch reviews typically include:

1. **Project summary** — What was commissioned and in which market(s)
2. **Scope** — Methodology, sample, timelines, languages
3. **Results** — Quality of deliverables and business impact
4. **Team** — Responsiveness, expertise, communication
5. **Recommendation** — Would they hire again for similar work

**Required regional signal (at least one per review):**
- Egypt: Cairo fieldwork, EDA/healthcare system context, Arabic qual
- UAE: Dubai/Abu Dhabi, DHA/DoH awareness, Emirates launch support
- KSA: Riyadh/Jeddah, SFDA-aware research, Vision 2030 healthcare themes

**Anchor text diversity note:** Reviews are user-generated; do not coach exact-match SEO phrases. Natural mentions of "market research in {{city/country}}" are sufficient.
