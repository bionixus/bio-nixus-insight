**Audience:** COO (primary), CEO (strategic deals), CFO (proposal-stage value)  
**Cadence:** Monthly — 12th of each month (Day 30 anchor from plan approval 12 Jun 2026)

---

## How to use

1. Duplicate this template into `docs/bd/gcc-pipeline-reports/YYYY-MM-gcc-pipeline-report-NN.md`.
2. Populate from CRM/account sheet ([BIO-72 enrichment](/BIO/issues/BIO-72)) and active deal records.
3. Post summary comment on [BIO-43](/BIO/issues/BIO-43) and attach the full report.
4. Hand to COO for review before any external distribution.

**Governance:** No pricing commitments without CFO sign-off. No delivery timeline promises without MRM capacity check.
