## Pre-Book Outreach Strategy

### Wave 1: T-60 (26 November 2026)
**Target:** 20 priority accounts (Segment A + B top picks)

**Approach:**
1. **Warm intro path (preferred):**
   - Leverage Global Health Exhibition (Riyadh Oct 2026) contacts
   - "Following up on our Riyadh conversation — let's continue in Dubai"
   - Use Sales Specialist for scheduling coordination

2. **Cold outreach path:**
   - LinkedIn InMail + email sequence
   - Subject: "GCC launch intelligence meeting — WHX Dubai Jan 2027"
   - Value prop: 30-min meeting to discuss GCC launch readiness for [specific asset]
   - CTA: "I'll be at WHX Dubai 25–28 Jan — are you attending? Happy to pre-book a meeting slot."

3. **Email template structure:**
   - Line 1: Event attendance confirmation + mutual context
   - Line 2: Specific value prop tied to their asset/market
   - Line 3: Meeting request + scheduling link
   - Line 4: Collateral attachment (launch landscape diagnostic teaser)

**Success metric:** 6–8 confirmed meetings by T-45 (11 December 2026)
