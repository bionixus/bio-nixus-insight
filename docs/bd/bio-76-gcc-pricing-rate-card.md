## Executive summary

This rate card formalises CFO-approved quote bands for GCC market research and business development proposals. Bands supersede indicative planning ranges in [BIO-43 §4.1–4.2](/BIO/issues/BIO-43#document-plan). **BDM may use these bands in scoped proposals immediately.** Outreach email pricing remains gated per [BIO-117](/BIO/issues/BIO-117) until supplemental CFO sign-off on template rate blocks.

**Financial lenses applied:** unit economics, contribution margin (land ≥35%, expand ≥40%), operating leverage, break-even analysis, scenario modeling (±15% labor sensitivity), opportunity cost (land vs expand pod allocation).

## §1 — Diagnostic / land tier

**Strategic label:** $28K–$75K (aggregate diagnostic tier including upper-bound HEOR-lite scoping)  
**Per-package land band:** $26K–$55K for standard land packages below.

| Package | Approved quote band (USD) | Target CM | Margin note |
|---------|---------------------------|-----------|-------------|
| GCC Access Snapshot | **$30,000 – $45,000** | ≥35% at floor | Floor raised +$2K vs plan; do not quote below $30K |
| Launch Landscape Diagnostic | **$38,000 – $55,000** | ≥35% at floor | Floor raised +$3K vs plan; reject at $35K |
| KOL Heatmap | **$32,000 – $50,000** | ≥35% at floor | Approved unchanged |
| Fieldwork-Only GCC | **$26,000 – $38,000** | ≥35% at floor | Floor raised +$4K vs plan; do not quote below $26K |

**Land tier guardrails**

- Cap interview / respondent counts in SOW; GCC field cost inflation (KSA/UAE) can erode CM 5–8 pts if scope creeps.
- Quote GCC logistics premium as a line item where applicable (+10% on variable field lines vs Egypt hub baseline).
- **Opportunity cost:** Under-priced land deals divert pod capacity from $95K+ expand work — enforce land-and-expand sequencing per COO.

## §2 — Expansion tier

**Strategic label:** $80K–$250K+  
**Target contribution margin:** ≥40% at approved floors.

| Package | Approved quote band (USD) | Target CM | Margin note |
|---------|---------------------------|-----------|-------------|
| Multi-Country GCC Launch Readiness | **$95,000 – $180,000** | ≥40% at floor | MRM sign-off required >$95K; phase 5+ markets |
| GCC Brand Tracking Programme | **$120,000 – $200,000 / year** | 66–80% | Approved unchanged |
| HEOR / Pharmacoeconomics GCC | **$65,000 – $120,000** | ≥40% at floor | Floor raised +$10K vs plan; hard floor $65K |
| Competitive Intelligence Programme | **$60,000 – $100,000 / year** | 55–73% | Approved unchanged |
| Patient Journey / PSP Research | **$80,000 – $130,000** | ≥40% at floor | Floor raised to align with expansion tier |

**Expansion tier guardrails**

- **Multi-country scope creep:** Do not discount Multi-Country Launch Readiness below **$95,000** without CEO approval; phase additional markets at expand rates.
- **Concurrent expand overload:** Enforce 2-slot cap per [BIO-78](/BIO/issues/BIO-78); price contractor surge ($8K–$12K/mo) into SOW when capacity tight.
- Annual programmes (Brand Tracking, Competitive Intelligence) require 12-month SOW with renewal terms stated at proposal.

## §3 — GCC margin risks & mitigations

| Risk | Financial impact | Required mitigation in quote / SOW |
|------|------------------|-------------------------------------|
| KSA/UAE field cost inflation | −5 to −8 pts contribution margin on qual land | GCC premium line item; cap interview count |
| Concurrent expand overload | Overtime / contractor surge erodes expand CM | 2-slot cap; surge pricing in SOW |
| Discount pressure on first deal | Land at plan floor → negative unit economics | Discount authority matrix (§5) |
| Multi-country scope creep | $95K floor becomes loss-leader | Phase pricing; tranche 2 at expand rates |
| Payroll actuals ≠ model | Bands may be ±10% off true CM | Finance variance analysis after first 3 GCC closes |

**Model assumptions (BIO-77):** Blended fully loaded GCC FTE-month **$9,500** (±15% sensitivity); variable field/recruitment **25–35%** of revenue (qual-heavy land), **20–28%** (quant fieldwork). Refresh when HR provides actual loaded FTE-month.

## §4 — Discount authority matrix

| Discount level | Authority | Conditions |
|----------------|-----------|------------|
| **0–10%** off list (approved band midpoint) | **BDM** | Document rationale in CRM; no margin exception |
| **>10% and ≤15%** off list | **CFO written approval** | Written email or issue comment with CM impact note |
| **>15% below approved package floor** | **CEO approval** | Strategic loss-leader only; CFO must attach CM scenario |
| **Any quote below approved floor** | **CEO approval** | Required regardless of discount % |
| **Multi-Country Launch Readiness < $95,000** | **CEO approval** | No exceptions without CEO sign-off |
| **HEOR < $65,000** | **Reject** | Hard floor — negative unit economics at $55K |

**List price definition:** Midpoint of the approved quote band for the scoped package, unless SOW specifies a fixed fee within band.

**Example:** Launch Landscape Diagnostic band $38K–$55K → list midpoint **$46,500**. A 12% discount ($40,920) requires CFO written approval. A quote at $36,000 (>15% below floor) requires CEO approval.
