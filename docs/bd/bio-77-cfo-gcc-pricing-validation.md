## Executive summary

1. **Diagnostic (land) tier $25K–$75K is directionally sound** but two package floors need revision before external quoting.
2. **Expansion tier $80K–$250K+ is approved** with one scope guardrail on multi-country programmes.
3. **GCC-specific margin risk:** Fieldwork-heavy land packages at plan floor prices fall below the 35% contribution-margin target; KSA/UAE logistics premium and respondent incentives are the primary drivers.

**Recommendation:** BDM may quote using the **approved bands in §4** immediately after [BIO-76](/BIO/issues/BIO-76) publishes the formal rate card. Until then, outreach remains pricing-free per [BIO-117](/BIO/issues/BIO-117).

## GCC-specific margin risks

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| **KSA/UAE field cost inflation** | −5 to −8 pts CM on qual land | Quote GCC premium line item; cap interview count in SOW |
| **Concurrent expand overload** | Overtime / contractor surge ($8K–$12K/mo) erodes expand CM | Enforce 2-slot cap per [BIO-78](mrm-q4-2026-gcc-capacity-report.md); price surge into SOW |
| **Discount pressure on first deal** | Land packages at plan floor → negative unit economics | CEO approval for any land quote **>15% below approved floor** |
| **Multi-country scope creep** | $95K floor becomes loss-leader at 5+ markets | Phase pricing; second tranche at expand rates |
| **Payroll actuals ≠ model** | Bands may be ±10% off true CM | Finance to reconcile after first 3 GCC closes (variance analysis) |

## Financial lenses applied

- **Unit economics:** Fieldwork-only and HEOR at plan floors fail margin test — floors revised.
- **Contribution margin:** Land ≥35%, expand ≥40% enforced on approved floors.
- **Operating leverage:** Expand packages absorb fixed pod cost; land deals must not subsidize delivery at scale.
- **Break-even analysis:** Access Snapshot break-even ~$21K (mid cost); plan $28K floor was only 27% CM — raised to $30K.
- **Scenario modeling:** Base case above; downside (+15% labor) triggers floor revisions in §3 sensitivity table.
- **Opportunity cost:** Under-priced land to win logos diverts pod from $95K+ expand work — COO to enforce land-and-expand sequencing.
