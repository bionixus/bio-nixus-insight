### Why pricing stays out of outreach

- **Unit economics:** Indicative bands in [BIO-43](/BIO/issues/BIO-43#document-plan) ($22K–$55K entry; $55K–$200K/year core) are planning inputs only — not margin-validated for external quote.
- **Operating leverage:** Publishing ranges before cost structure sign-off ([BIO-77](/BIO/issues/BIO-77)) creates downstream discount pressure and proposal rework.
- **Scenario modeling:** Outreach should drive discovery calls; pricing belongs in scoped proposals after rate-card approval.

### Approved operational language (replace pricing placeholders in live templates)

> *Pricing and scope bands are shared after a brief discovery call — we do not quote fixed fees in initial outreach.*

Or simply **delete** the pricing bullet/line with no replacement.

### Path to unblock pricing in outreach (separate from this sign-off)

1. Complete [BIO-77](/BIO/issues/BIO-77) margin validation on diagnostic ($25K–$75K) and expansion ($80K–$250K+) tiers.
2. Publish approved rate card via [BIO-76](/BIO/issues/BIO-76).
3. CFO will issue a **supplemental sign-off** authorising a single approved rate block for Sequence A T5 / Sequence C T3 only.

Until step 3, **CEO sign-off required** for any exception to the remove-until-approved rule.
