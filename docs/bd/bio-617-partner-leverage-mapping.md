## Warm Outreach Messaging Strategy

### Core Message Framework

**Subject Line Options:**
1. "Following up from Riyadh — let's meet in Dubai (WHX Jan 2027)"
2. "Great connecting at Global Health — continuing our conversation in Dubai?"
3. "[Company Name] GCC launch discussion — WHX Dubai follow-up"

**Email Template (Warm Intro Path):**

```
Hi [First Name],

Great speaking with you at the Global Health Exhibition in Riyadh last month — I enjoyed learning about [specific detail from Riyadh conversation, e.g., "your team's plans for the [Asset Name] launch in Saudi"].

I'll be attending the World Health Expo in Dubai (Jan 25–28, 2027) and wanted to continue our discussion around [specific topic, e.g., "GCC payer landscape insights" or "launch readiness diagnostic"]. 

Would you be interested in a 30-minute meeting during the event? I have slots available on [Date 1] or [Date 2].

Looking forward to reconnecting in Dubai.

Best regards,  
[Name]  
BioNixus  
[LinkedIn] | [Scheduling Link]
```

**Key messaging principles:**
1. **Specific recall:** Reference a memorable detail from Riyadh (demonstrates genuine relationship)
2. **Value continuity:** Frame Dubai meeting as "next step" in ongoing conversation
3. **Low friction:** Offer 2–3 specific time slots (reduce back-and-forth)
4. **Soft CTA:** "Interested in meeting?" vs "Please confirm" (warm tone)
