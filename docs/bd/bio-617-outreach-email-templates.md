## Overview

**Purpose:** Standardized email templates for WHX Dubai 2027 pre-booking outreach  
**Usage:** Sales Specialist executes Wave 1 (T-60) and Wave 2 (T-30) using these templates  
**Customization:** Segment-specific templates (A: GCC Launch, B: RWD, C: Consulting Partners)  

**Template Philosophy:**
- **Specificity:** Reference the recipient's company/asset/market (no generic spray-and-pray)
- **Value-first:** Lead with "what we can help with" not "who we are"
- **Low friction:** Clear CTA, scheduling link, 30-min time commitment
- **Social proof:** Reference existing meetings/relationships where applicable

If sending >50 emails total, consider A/B testing subject lines:

| Version A (Direct) | Version B (Value-First) | Hypothesis |
|--------------------|-------------------------|------------|
| "Meeting request — WHX Dubai Jan 2027" | "GCC launch intelligence meeting — WHX Dubai" | B has higher open rate (specific value prop) |

- Measure open rate and reply rate after 7 days
