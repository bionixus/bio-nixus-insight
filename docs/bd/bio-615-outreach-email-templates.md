## Operational Checklist (Per Template)

### Before each send
- [ ] COO/CMO approval recorded (T-90, T-45 emails)
- [ ] All personalization tokens mapped to CRM contact records
- [ ] No `{{placeholder}}` tokens, `[SOURCE NEEDED]`, or drafting brackets in send queue
- [ ] Account brief complete for Sequence B accounts (BIO-72 briefs confirmed)
- [ ] `{{calendar_link}}` live and UTM-tracked
- [ ] `{{one_pager_link}}` published (T-30 email)
- [ ] Attachments ready (one-pager PDF, calendar .ics file, proof points card)
- [ ] Sender name/title matches approved NAP (Business Development for operational; CEO/COO for strategic)

### Per-touch logging (Sales Specialist / BDM)
- [ ] Send date recorded in CRM
- [ ] Channel logged (email, LinkedIn)
- [ ] Reply / meeting outcome captured
- [ ] Next touch scheduled or sequence exited
- [ ] BANT qualification applied for post-meeting follow-ups

### Exit rules
- **Positive reply at any touch:** Exit sequence; move to discovery / qualified stage
- **"Not now" reply:** Pause 90 days; add to nurture list
- **Opt-out / unsubscribe:** Remove from all sequences immediately
- **Meeting confirmed at T-90 or T-60:** Skip to T-30 confirmation sequence
- **No response by T-30:** Final attempt at T-14; otherwise deprioritize for this event
