## Methodology

- **Sources:** Official regulators (MOHAP/EDE, DoH, CMS, G-BA, HAS, BOE, gov.br CONITEC, NMPA/CDE summaries), EC HTA pages (for DE/EU cross-reference only), industry legal guides (Chambers UAE 2026, Simon-Kucher LFSS, Cratia EAEU).

- **Recency:** Primary citations 2024–2026; EAEU transition and UAE EDE transfer are 2025–2026 developments.
- **Limitations:** No paid IQVIA/Evaluate access; UAE/China market $ figures show publisher variance; Russia figures FX-sensitive.
