## Methodology

- **Sources:** Official regulators (EC, FDA, G-BA, BOE, NMPA-adjacent guidance), government budget documents (KPMG on Saudi 2026 budget), industry consultancies (IQVIA, Simon-Kucher, CRA) for interpretive context only.
- **Triangulation:** Minimum 2 independent sources for each quantitative claim recommended for body copy.
- **Recency:** All primary citations dated 2024–2026; flag anything older than 18 months.
- **Limitations:** Paid syndicated market reports (Evaluate, GlobalData) not accessed; Brazil/China/Russia market sizes show material variance across publishers — use ranges only.
