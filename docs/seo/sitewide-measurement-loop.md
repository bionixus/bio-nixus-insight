# Sitewide Measurement Loop (Biweekly)

## Cadence
- **Weekly**: KPI capture and alert scan.
- **Biweekly**: iteration decisions for titles, opening copy, and internal links.
- **Monthly**: full cluster review and thin-page rescoring.

## KPI Set
- Average position by target page and keyword cluster.
- CTR by page title/meta variant.
- Organic sessions by strategic route group.
- Lead actions (contact submits, proposal clicks, call clicks).
- Conversion rate by page family (market, access, company-intent).

## Iteration Triggers
- CTR below baseline for 2 consecutive weeks -> test title variant.
- Position stagnates outside top 10 for 4 weeks -> strengthen intro + proof blocks.
- High impressions, low conversion -> tighten CTA and execution-path links.

## Route Families to Track
- Market/BOFU pages
- Access/strategic service pages
- Country-intent EN + AR pages
- Company-intent pages

## Biweekly Decision Log Template
- **Page URL**
- **Observed issue**
- **Hypothesis**
- **Change deployed**
- **Expected KPI impact**
- **Review date**
