# Executive English Style Standard

## Audience
- Marketing Directors
- Commercial and Market Access Leads
- CEOs and GM-level readers

## Required Tone
- Simple, direct, and professional.
- No jargon-heavy wording where plain alternatives exist.
- Decision-oriented language over descriptive filler.

## Section Writing Format
1. **Why it matters**
2. **What the evidence says**
3. **What to do next**

## Sentence Rules
- Prefer short sentences.
- Keep one idea per sentence.
- Use active voice.
- Avoid nested clauses when a simple sentence works.

## Vocabulary Rules
- Prefer common business words over technical abstractions.
- Explain technical terms once, then use plain terms.
- Use measurable terms when possible (time range, percentage range, milestone window).

## Layout Rules
- One clear H1.
- H2/H3 sections that are skimmable.
- Bullets for execution steps and KPI highlights.
- End with CTA that reflects the page intent.
