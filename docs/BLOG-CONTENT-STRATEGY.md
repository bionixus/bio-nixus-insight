## Content Guidelines

1. **Tone**: Corporate & premium (McKinsey/IQVIA style). Authoritative, data-driven, no fluff.
2. **Length**: 1,200–2,500 words per post. Longer for evergreen guides.
3. **Structure**: Clear H2/H3 headings, bullet points, data callouts, and a strong CTA at the end.
4. **Internal Links**: Every post should link to at least 2 relevant service pages and 1 case study.
5. **CTA**: End every post with "Request a Proposal" or "Contact BioNixus" call-to-action.
6. **Images**: Use relevant stock imagery or data visualisations. Optimise for WebP format.
7. **AI Assistance**: Use AI to draft, but have a domain expert review for clinical accuracy.

## Distribution

- Publish on Sanity CMS via the existing blog infrastructure
- Share on LinkedIn company page within 24 hours of publishing
- Include in monthly newsletter to subscriber list
