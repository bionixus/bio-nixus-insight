## Sales commission structure

- Commission rate: 5% of collected revenue
- Payable after cash receipt and monthly close by CFO
- Exclusions: taxes, pass-through data and panel costs, and reimbursed travel
- Acceleration trigger: individual deals closed ahead of a 5-week sales cycle
- Cap override: no commission is paid on revenue below minimum project margin of 70%

## CAC assumptions

- Target CAC: less than $8,000 per new client
- CAC denominator: digital spend plus event spend plus deal-support travel
- CAC numerator: new logos with first paid invoice in that calendar month

## Monthly payment, pipeline, and win rate targets

- Monthly marketing payment review on the first business day of each month
- Q1 target pays: 2 to 3 projects per month
- Pipeline coverage target by month end: 3x new pipeline value for next quarter revenue target
- Win rate target by month end: greater than 30%

## Role in company marketing execution

This plan supports the approved marketing roles and accountabilities from BIO-174:

- Maintain quarterly content calendar tied to therapy areas and industry events
- Run closed-loop marketing measurement and share CAC, pipeline, and win rate reporting with CFO
