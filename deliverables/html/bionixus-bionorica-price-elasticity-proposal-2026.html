Dear Dr. ElFaramawy,

Thank you for sharing your brand coverage matrix and the request for three quotation levels per brand — 100, 200, and 300 physicians — at USD 250 all-in per completed physician session. This proposal responds with a mixed-methods programme that pairs qualitative depth (prescribing narratives, substitution logic, channel friction) with quantitative price instruments (Gabor-Granger / PSM / discrete choice).

Bionorica’s portfolio spans mature ENT, pediatric, and urology franchises plus a pre-launch Bronchipret module. Per your direction, Sinupret Syrup and Bronchipret are conducted in one combined pediatric session with the same PED specialists (~65–75 minutes), while Sinupret Forte (ENT) and Canephron (URO) remain separate specialty sessions. Our design respects Egypt’s dual MOH–private channel reality and moderator neutrality — client target prices are not disclosed during qualitative warm-up.

Below you will find programme objectives, the four-brand matrix, methodology summary, three investment tiers ($75k / $150k / $225k programme totals), an eight-week indicative timeline, and next steps. We recommend Tier B (200 physicians per brand) for stable specialty-level themes and robust subgroup cuts unless budget requires Tier A.

We would welcome the assignment and stand ready to align field calendars with your commercial timeline.

02 — Mixed-methods design

One session,
qual depth + quant elasticity.

Each physician session combines qualitative depth and standardized quantitative price modules. Pediatric sessions cover Sinupret Syrup and Bronchipret together with the same specialist; ENT and URO sessions are brand-specific. Moderators are blinded to Bionorica target prices during qual warm-up.
