## What is BioNixus?

BioNixus is a global pharmaceutical and healthcare market research company. Founded 2012. Headquartered in the United States with offices in the United Kingdom.

BioNixus specialises in primary market research for pharmaceutical, biotech, and medtech companies across MENA, Latin America, and EMEA.

## Brazil Regulatory Context

ANVISA (Agência Nacional de Vigilância Sanitária) — federal drug and device regulator
CMED (Câmara de Regulação do Mercado de Medicamentos) — maximum price regulator
CONITEC (Comissão Nacional de Incorporação de Tecnologias no SUS) — HTA for SUS reimbursement
ANS (Agência Nacional de Saúde Suplementar) — private health insurance regulator
CONEP/CEP — ethics review for patient research (Resolution CNS 510/2016)
LGPD/ANPD — data protection law and authority
CFM/CRM — Federal and Regional Council of Medicine (physician regulation)
INTERFARMA — pharmaceutical industry self-regulatory association
