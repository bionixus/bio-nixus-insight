Service Portfolio

1. Market Sizing & Forecasting

We employ multiple methodological approaches to triangulate market size estimates, reducing uncertainty and building stakeholder confidence:

  • Epidemiological modeling for patient population estimates
  • Prescription data analysis for current market assessment
  • Analog forecasting leveraging comparable markets and products
  • Time-series analysis for trend projection

Typical applications: Launch planning, investment cases, market entry decisions

2. Pricing & Market Access Research

Understanding willingness-to-pay, price sensitivity, and payer perspectives requires sophisticated quantitative techniques:

  • Conjoint analysis for attribute trade-off quantification
  • Van Westendorp Price Sensitivity Meter for optimal pricing ranges
  • Discrete choice modeling for reimbursement scenario planning
  • Budget impact modeling for payer value demonstration

Typical applications: Pricing strategy, reimbursement dossiers, health economics outcomes research (HEOR)

3. Physician & Patient Surveys

Large-scale surveying with statistical power to detect meaningful differences and segment markets:

  • Sample size determination based on precision requirements
  • Stratified sampling ensuring representative coverage across therapeutic areas and geographies
  • Multi-mode data collection (online, telephone, face-to-face) optimized for response rates
  • Advanced analytics including factor analysis, cluster segmentation, and predictive modeling

Typical applications: Brand perception tracking, unmet needs assessment, treatment pathway analysis

4. Clinical Trial Feasibility

Quantitative assessment of site capability, patient availability, and operational requirements:

  • Site capacity surveys with statistical validation
  • Patient flow modeling for enrollment projections
  • Competitive trial mapping for feasibility assessment
  • Investigator profiling with quantitative scoring

Typical applications: Protocol design, site selection, enrollment forecasting

Case Application: Biologics Market Assessment

Challenge:

A multinational pharmaceutical company required precise market sizing for a novel biologic entering GCC dermatology markets, with specific focus on psoriasis and atopic dermatitis.

Approach:

  • Epidemiological analysis of target patient populations across UAE, Saudi Arabia, and Kuwait
  • Quantitative physician surveys (n=180 dermatologists) assessing current treatment patterns and unmet needs
  • Prescription data analysis validating market size estimates
  • Price sensitivity research informing market access strategy

Outcome:

  • Validated addressable market estimate with ±8% confidence interval
  • Identified three distinct physician segments with differentiated targeting implications
  • Quantified price-performance trade-offs informing launch pricing strategy
  • Delivered actionable insights supporting $15M market entry decision
